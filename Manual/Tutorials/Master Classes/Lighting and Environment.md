## Content

In this master class, lighting artist Lars Hofrichter takes you on a deep dive into the beauty of the CRYENGINE lighting and environment tools, providing expert tips to configure interdependent settings for optimal results that you won't find anywhere else. In under 90 minutes, Lars walks you through every Environment Editor setting including SVOGI and volumetric fog, relevant console variables, setup for interior and exterior lighting, lighting and fog components, sky domes, clip volumes, hidden settings, and more.
