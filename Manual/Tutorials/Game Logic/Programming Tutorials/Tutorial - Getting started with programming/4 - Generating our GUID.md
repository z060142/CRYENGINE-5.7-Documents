##
Generating our GUID

Now, we need to provide our plug-in with a name and a GUID:

-
Access the
**
Create GUID
**
 from entering the
**
Tools
**
menu.

-
In the message box, choose
**
Registry Format
**
.

-
Next, press
**
Copy
**
, and paste our generated GUID into our code.
