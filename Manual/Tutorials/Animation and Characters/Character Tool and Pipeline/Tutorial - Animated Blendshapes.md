## Overview

This article covers how to create and implement animated Blendshapes for CRYENGINE.

Blendshapes or morph targets (the terms are interchangeable) are 3D mesh shapes of a model with the same mesh topology. They are primarily used for character facial animation or to fix a specific (bad) deformation state. They are usually either modeled by artists or by scanning physical models, like a human actor's face, and post-processed by software and artists to extract the geometry data. In this tutorial series we concentrate on Blendshapes used for animation and how to get them into CRYENGINE.
