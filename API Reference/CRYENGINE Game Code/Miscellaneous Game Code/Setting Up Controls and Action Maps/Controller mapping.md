## Content

### Overview

The mapping for in-game input controller has been updated are following standard first person game controls.

For PC an Xbox 360 gamepad can be connected on USB ports.

### Default Mappings

Player Action | PC | 360 | PS3
--- | --- | --- | ---
**Player Movement** | W, A, S, D | Left Stick | Left Stick
**Player Aim** | Mouse XY | Right Stick | Right Stick
**Jump** | Spacebar | A | Cross
**Sprint** | Shift | Left Stick (click) | Left Stick (click)
**Crouch** | C | B | Circle
**Slide** (when sprinting) | C | B | Circle
**Fire** | Mouse 1 | RT | R1
**Zoom** | Mouse 2 | LT | L1
**Melee** | V | Right Stick (click) | Right Stick (click)
**Firemode** | 2 | D-Pad Left | Pad Left
**Reload** | R | X | Square
**Use** | F | X (hold) | Square (hold)
**Toggle Weapon** | 1 | Y | Triangle
**Toggle Explosive** | 3 | D-Pad Right | Pad Right
**Toggle Binoculars** | B | D-Pad Up | D-Pad Up
**Toggle Light** (attachment) | L | D-Pad Down | D-Pad Down
**Third Person Camera** | F1 | Back | Select
Vehicle Action | PC | 360 | PS3
**Accelerate** | W | RT | R2
**Boost** | Shift | A | Cross
**Brake/Reverse** | S | LT | L2
**Handbrake** | Spacebar | B | Circle
**Steer** | A/D | Left Stick | Left Stick
**Look** | Mouse XY | Right Stick | Right Stick
**Horn** | H | Right Stick (click) | Right Stick (click)
**Fire** | Mouse 1 | RB | R1
**Change Seat** | C | D-Pad Up | D-Pad Up
**Headlights** | L | D-Pad Down | D-Pad Down
Helicopter Action | PC | 360 | PS3
**Ascend** | W | RT | R2
**Descend** | S | LT | L2
**Roll Left** | A | Left Stick (left) | Left Stick (left)
**Roll Right** | D | Left Stick (right) | Left Stick (right)
**Yaw Left** | Mouse X (left) | Right Stick (left) | Right Stick (left)
**Yaw Right** | Mouse X (right) | Right Stick (right) | Right Stick (right)
**Pitch Up** | Mouse Y (up) | Left Stick (down) | Left Stick (down)
**Pitch Down** | Mouse Y (down) | Left Stick (up) | Left Stick (up)
Multiplayer | PC | 360 | PS3
**Show Scoreboard** | TAB | Back (hold) | Select (hold)

### Reconfiguring controls

It's possible to reconfigure the controls by following instructions in [Setting Up Controls and Action Maps](../Setting%20Up%20Controls%20and%20Action%20Maps.md).
